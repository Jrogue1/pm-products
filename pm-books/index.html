Questions

Frequently Asked

What does "self-hosted" mean? ▼
PM Books runs on your own machine or server. Your financial data never touches a cloud service. You install it, you run it, you control it. Works on any system with Node.js 20+.
How does the AI work without cloud? ▼
PM Books uses Venice AI, a zero-data-retention provider. Your prompts are processed and immediately discarded. Nothing is stored, nothing is used for training. You can also use local AI models for fully offline operation.
Do I need accounting knowledge? ▼
No. The setup wizard generates everything based on your business type. The AI handles categorization and account mapping. Describe expenses in plain English and PM Books does the rest.
Can I use this for taxes? ▼
Yes. Pro includes Schedule C export and tax-ready reports. Every transaction maps to an IRS category. Export what your accountant needs in one click. This is bookkeeping software, not tax filing, but it gives your accountant exactly what they need.
Is there a monthly fee? ▼
Your choice: one-time payment or monthly. Same software either way. The one-time price is better if you'll use it more than a few months.
How does the Telegram bot work? ▼
Pro includes a Telegram bot. Send "lunch $12 at Chipotle" and it creates a categorized transaction. Send receipt photos for OCR. Check balances, pull reports, all from your phone.
What if I need help migrating data? ▼
PM Books supports CSV import at about 1,000 transactions per second. For complex migrations, add the guided setup session and we'll handle the import alongside your full configuration.
